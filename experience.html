My Learning Journey

2019-2020
The Awakening - Realizing My Passion
Began exploring technology seriously while working traditional jobs. Started understanding that my real passion was in creating digital experiences that bring people together rather than drive them apart. Began initial research into game development and educational technology.
2021-2022
Deep Learning Phase - Foundation Building
Intensive self-study in programming fundamentals (Python, JavaScript), cybersecurity principles, and game development concepts. Spent countless evenings after work learning through online courses, tutorials, and hands-on experimentation. This wasn't casual learning - this was preparation for my future.
2023
Project Conceptualization - Turning Ideas Into Plans
Developed comprehensive concepts for WorldweaveMMO, Wonder Labs Kids educational platform, and party rental business. Learned project management, business planning, and began understanding how to turn passionate ideas into actionable plans. Started engaging with gaming communities to understand real user needs.
2024
Prototype Development - Making It Real
Began actual development work on game prototypes, deepened understanding of Godot and Unity engines, and started creating functional systems rather than just concepts. Joined Fantage OS community as a beta tester (though official testing hasn't begun yet). This year was about proving to myself that I could build, not just dream.
2025
Professional Launch - From Learning to Serving
Made the decision to leave traditional employment and commit full-time to building Kinex Ventures. This isn't about having "arrived" - it's about being ready to apply years of preparation in service of real clients with real needs. The learning continues, but now it happens alongside building solutions for others.
