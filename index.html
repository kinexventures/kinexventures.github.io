The Founder's Journey

From Personal Transformation to Revolutionary Technology

I'm not just working towards building an empire - I'm working towards building the better world I want to not just dream about but live in with my children. As a single father channeling profound personal transformation into systematic solutions for children's development, I'm not just creating technology; I'm building my daughters' future and every child's opportunity for integrated growth.

My journey through challenges - including time in jail and complex relationship dynamics - sparked a conscious decision in 2020 to become the person my daughters deserve. That transformation isn't just my story; it's the foundation of authentic leadership that refuses to accept systemic limitations on children's potential.

Every line of code, every business decision, every community program through LJ's Crew (honoring my grandfather's legacy) serves one mission: proving that technology built with genuine stakes creates revolutionary outcomes for humanity's most important investment - our children.
